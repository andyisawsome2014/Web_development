Running Time for Sorting Algorithms

  • Bubble Sort: O(n^2)
  • Insertion Sort: O(n^2)
  • Quick Sort: O(n^2);
    • Average O(nlogn)
  • Selection Sort: O(n^2)
  • Heap Sort: O(nlogn);
    • Heapify O(n)
